# ADSYS

## Disaster Support and Assistance System

ADSYS is a web-based disaster management platform designed to improve disaster preparedness, coordinate relief efforts, and support communities before, during, and after disasters.

---

##  Problem Definition

Natural disasters such as earthquakes, floods, wildfires, and landslides often create challenges in accessing reliable information, organizing donations, coordinating volunteers, and managing emergency resources. Existing disaster-related services are generally distributed across multiple platforms, making it difficult for users to quickly access the support they need.

---

##  Project Purpose

The aim of ADSYS is to provide a centralized disaster management platform where users can prepare for emergencies, explore disaster supply products, participate in verified donation campaigns, apply as volunteers, locate donation centers, and access disaster awareness resources through a single user-friendly interface.

##  Disaster Preparedness Checklist

The Disaster Preparedness Checklist is one of the core features of ADSYS, designed to help users evaluate their readiness for potential disasters. Users can complete an interactive checklist covering essential emergency preparations, including emergency kits, first aid supplies, meeting plans, emergency contacts, power banks, flashlights, and food and water supplies.

The system automatically calculates a preparedness percentage based on the completed items, allowing users to easily identify missing preparations and improve their overall disaster readiness.

##  Disaster Store

The Disaster Store provides a dedicated marketplace for disaster preparedness products, enabling users to explore essential emergency supplies before disasters occur. The store includes a wide range of products such as first aid kits, emergency food, bottled water, flashlights, power banks, hygiene products, emergency blankets, fire extinguishers, whistles, and other survival equipment.

Users can browse products by category, view detailed product information, add products to their favorites, manage their shopping cart, and easily access items that support disaster preparedness.

## Admin Panel

The Admin Panel provides centralized management for the ADSYS platform. Authorized administrators can manage products, product categories, donation campaigns, donation centers, volunteer applications, user records, orders, and disaster-related content through a secure management interface.

This panel makes the system easier to maintain and allows platform data to be updated without directly modifying the source code.
